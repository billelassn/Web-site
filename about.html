Hello! My name is Billel Aissani.

From a strong background in finance and data analysis to a passion for AI in healthcare.

As a graduate engineer from Mines Saint-Étienne and Université Paris Dauphine - PSL, I've developed solid expertise in data analysis, statistical modeling, and optimization. I've successfully applied these skills as a Pricing and Data Analyst in financial and insurance sectors, specializing in risk modeling and data transformation.

My professional experience awakened a desire to give a more human meaning to my skills. This led me to a passionate career pivot towards Artificial Intelligence, particularly in the healthcare sector. I've strengthened my expertise through an AI in Health Certificate at CNAM Paris and my current enrollment in the ST4H (Sciences & Technology for Health) Master's program at Université PSL.

I am deeply passionate about leveraging AI to accelerate scientific discoveries, from cheminformatics and drug design to diagnostics. I've applied these interests in real-world challenges like the Owkin challenge for PIK3CA mutation detection and my current role as a Data Scientist at Nepita, where I'm working with cutting-edge volatomics technology for disease diagnosis.

I am actively seeking to connect with professionals who are passionate about using AI to revolutionize healthcare and solve urgent global challenges.
